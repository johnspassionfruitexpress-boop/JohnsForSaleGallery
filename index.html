Quick Care Guide

Simple care tips to help your plant settle in after pickup.

☀️ Sun

Passion fruit loves bright light and strong sun once adjusted.

💧 Water

Keep evenly moist, but never leave roots sitting in standing water.

🌿 Support

Give the vine a trellis, fence, arch, or frame to climb.

🪴 Soil

Use well-draining soil for healthier roots and better growth.

❄️ Cold

Protect young plants during cold snaps or freeze warnings.

🌱 Adjust

Give the plant a few days to settle before heavy feeding.

Basic Care After Purchase

  • Sun: Best with strong light and plenty of sun once adjusted.
  • Water: Keep evenly moist, but do not leave sitting in standing water.
  • Support: Give the vine something to climb as it grows.
  • Soil: Use well-draining soil. Passion fruit does not like soggy roots.
  • Cold protection: Protect young plants during cold snaps or freeze warnings.
  • Transplanting: Give it a few days to adjust before heavy feeding or major changes.

Basic Care After Purchase

  • Sun: Passion fruit loves bright light and strong sun once adjusted.
  • Water: Keep evenly moist, but do not let the roots sit in standing water.
  • Support: Give the vine something to climb such as a fence, trellis, arch, or frame.
  • Soil: Use well-draining soil. Healthy roots need air as much as moisture.
  • Cold protection: Protect young plants during cold snaps or freeze warnings.
  • Adjustment time: Give your plant a few days to settle before heavy feeding or major changes.

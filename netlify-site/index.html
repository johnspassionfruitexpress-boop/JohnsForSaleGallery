Quick Care Guide

Simple care tips to help your passion fruit plant adjust after pickup.

☀️ Sun

Passion fruit loves bright light and strong sun once adjusted.

💧 Water

Keep moist, but never let the roots sit in standing water.

🌿 Support

Give the vine a trellis, arch, fence, or frame to climb.

🪴 Soil

Use well-draining soil for healthier roots.

❄️ Cold

Protect young plants during cold snaps or freeze warnings.

🌱 Adjustment

Give your plant a few days to settle before heavy feeding.
